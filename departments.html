Computer Scientist

What kind of work is done?

Mainly the study of computers. Computer Scientist deal mostly with software and software systems. They work around developing new systems, technologies as well as computer based solutions (theory, development, design, development and application). Computer Scientist also conduct new research to help develop new computer technologies to solve various problems in a wide range of fields.

What kinds of people does they interact with?

Computer Scientist work with government agencies and private software publishers. They also work as part of a research team which involve computer programmers, I.T professionals as well as electrical and/or mechanical engineers.

Where do they spend most of their time?

A Computer Scientist often spends their time working at a firm or company, often work as part of a research team with computer programmers, I.T professionals and electrical or mechanical engineers.

What aspect of their position is most challenging?

Computer Scientists are often faced with Encryption and quantum computing, Algorithmic Bias, Security in the IOT and Artificial Intelligence.

Software Developer

What kind of work is done?

Run, design and test various computer programs and applications. Being a Software Developer involves being a great problem solver. In more details, key task include:

  • Participating in code review and design
  • Executing software development tasks
  • Maintaining and establishing project builds
    • What kinds of people does they interact with?

    Software Developers interact with their fellow Software Development Team.

    Where do they spend most of their time?

    Software Developers spend most of their time within the company or firm. Some work as a freelancer.

    What aspect of their position is most challenging?

    While solving problems can be satisfying to a Computer Scientist, is can be very frustrating when solutions to a problem arises. The longer it takes to solve a problem, the more frustrating it can get. These challenging aspects include, having unspecific requirements, poor documentation, inefficient development processes and outdated technology.

    Web Developer

    What kind of work is done?

    Web Developers design, create and modify websites. They are mainly responsible for maintaining a user friendly, stable website that offers the necessary functionality for their clients needs. At the end of the day, A Web Developer strives to create an appealing visual site that features clear navigation and user friendly design. Web Developer Tasks include:

  • Building websites with HTML, CSS and JavaScript
  • Modifying and updating websites (according to a clientâ€™s or company specification
  • Collaborating with other colleagues
  • Testing websites (being able to troubleshoot any problems as soon as identified.

    • Web Developers have different roles. There is a front-end developer as well as a back-end developer. Front-end developers main responsibilities are responsible for producing aspects of a website which users can see an interact with, whereas back-end developers are tasked with the development of the underlying platforms which drive the front-end.

    What kinds of people does they interact with?

    Web Developers work with other colleagues. These may include copywriters,, graphic designers, content creators, I.T staff and marketing managers.Also associate with external clients.

    Where do they spend most of their time?

    Web Developers usually work for a company, firm or a specific web design agency. Some Web Developers offer their services on a freelance basis.

    What aspect of their position is most challenging?

    Internet Explorer is by far one of the biggest challenges that Web Developers face. Even if the website looks perfect in every other browser, Internet Explorer always has quirks which need to be addressed. Other challenges also include task automation and performance.

    Business Analyst

    What kind of work is done?

    A Business analyst Conducts market analyses, analysing product lines and overall profitability of the business. A business Analyst has the responsibility of knowing and identifying when a businessâ€™s needs change, able to assess the impact of those business changes, obtaining, recording and examining requirements, and maintaining the communication as well as the delivery of the necessary requirements to stakeholders.
